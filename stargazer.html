MapleStory STARGAZER Promotional Trailers

I did two trailers for MapleStory's STARGAZER update, which promoted the new features coming to the game. For Part 1, stakeholders wanted a video that was representative of a rock concert and the new character Sia, which heavily foxuses on stars and galaxy themes. For Part 2, stakeholders wanted a video that was focused on sprite animations and beachy themes to match the summer release date. I handled all the motion graphics, scene layouts, background creation, transitions, visual narrative execution, and animations.
