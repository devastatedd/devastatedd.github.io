MapleStory STARGAZER Promotional Trailers

I did two trailers for MapleStory's STARGAZER update, which promoted the new features coming to the game. I handled all the motion graphics, scene layouts, background creation, transitions, visual narrative execution, and animations.

Part 1

For Part 1, stakeholders wanted a video that was representative of a rock concert and the new Maplestory character Sia, who is themed after stars and constellations.


Part 2

For Part 2, stakeholders wanted a video that was focused on sprite animations and beachy/travel themes to match the summer release date.
