Twitch Stream Pop-Up Toggles

"Pop Up Toggles" are Twitch stream toggles that I came up with to make it easier for new viewers to learn about a streamer from a raid or to remind people of chat rules. They are visually more fun to look at compared to a bot automated chat reminder because they "pop up" (hence the name) on stream and are custom designed to fit the client's aesthetic. A base character art is provided by the client and I handle all aspects of design and animation surrounding the art.
