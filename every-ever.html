Suiting Up

Animated and designed the title slate and main template for IGN's Every Ever, a video series that goes over every _____ ever (Avengers, X-Men, etc).

Logo and Main Title Scene

Since the client did not have any creative direction at the start of the project, the IGN Sales Design and Video team decided to try out two different looks to present to the client: A) a mechanical, gritty, weathered down look B) a look inspired by futuristic HUD elements/interface. I was tasked with the futuristic interface look.


Below are first drafts exploring a logo for the show. I started off with something bold and techy while using visual elements taken from Anthem's javelins just to get a feel for what direction I wanted with the logo. I then trailed off to thinner fonts that would compliment a HUD interface more without overpowering it.


Creating the HUD interface and seeing how it looked with the logo explorations I referenced reticles, FPS video game UI, and Anthem's gameplay UI.

Color change to match Anthem's orange more.

Upon review, the client came back with that they wanted the show to be branded more as Anthem rather than an IGN show with its own branding. Below is how the intro turned out using Anthem's pink/orange gradient with my HUD interface. Includes the animated version with an editable episode slate for Video team's use.
