Daily UI

Daily UI offers daily prompts that exercise your UI design skills. I used this challenge as a way to learn Sketch and get my feet wet in the UI, which I had not done so before. Below are the prompts I was able to complete in 2017 using Sketch, Principle, and After Effects.

Day 1 - Sign Up Page

Inspired by Voltron, I made a page to join Voltron's efforts in fighting the Galra.

Day 2 - Credit Card Checkout

Inspired by purchasing anime prints online. Wanted to also illustrate a different way customers could review and edit their orders.

Day 3 - Landing Page

Redesign of the Tokyo Ghoul Season 1 anime site.

Day 6 - User/Character Profile

Inspired by Psycho Pass.

Day 7 - Settings

A redesign of Persona 4's skill settings screen where you can reorder the skills in a swipe.

Day 8 - 404 Page

Used separate visual elements taken from Voltron's website and composited it into this scene.

Day 9 - Music Player

An anime app where you could click titles and listen to their soundtracks and then favorite the songs you want into your own personal playlist.

Day 13 - Direct Messaging

An update to Tumblr's mobile messaging system implementing elements I would love to see it have: group chats, more timestamps, night mode, and some of XKit’s and Discord’s customizable settings. Also consolidating your chat history/users you're talking to into one place rather than two separate windows on opposite ends of the screen like Tumblr currently has it.
