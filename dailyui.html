Daily UI

Daily UI offers daily prompts that exercise your UI design skills. Below are the prompts I was able to complete in 2017 using Sketch, Principle, and After Effects.

Day 1 - Sign Up Page

Day 2 - Credit Card Checkout. Wanted to also illustrate a way customers could review and edit their orders.

Day 3 - Landing Page. Redesign of the Tokyo Ghoul Season 1 anime site.

Day 6 - User/Character Profile

Day 7 - Settings. Redesign of Persona 4's skill settings screen where you can reorder the skills in a swipe.

Day 8 - 404 Page. Used visual elements taken from Voltron's website and composited it into this scene.

Day 9 - Music Player. An anime app where you could click titles and listen to their soundtracks and then favorite the songs you want into your own personal playlist.

Day 13 - Direct Messaging. An update to tumblr's mobile messaging system that has group chats, more timestamps, night mode, and some of XKit’s and Discord’s customizable settings
