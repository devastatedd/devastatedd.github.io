Intro

Once the logo was finalized by our senior designer (Justin Vachon), I was tasked to animate an intro with it. I took inspiration from the beer and bubble motifs and so animated the cup filling up and foaming, as well as the logo itself filling up. Bubbles were then used on the end of the intro to serve as a transition into the show.
