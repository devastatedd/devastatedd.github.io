Party Chat is a talkback show where the hosts read user comments, answer the audiences burning questions and hot takes, run polls and quizzes and play games with the audience. Very casual and very comedic. This show highlights the positivity in the community and ignores the negative.

Intro

Once the logo was finalized, I was tasked to animate an intro with it. I was inspired by the show's idea of community and fan interaction, so I made several copies of the speech bubbles to create the image of texting back and forth, quickly going into chaos.


Transition

Created a transition to use between cuts that draws upon the intro's community and messaging theme.


Lower Thirds & Name Bars

Lower thirds and name bars editors could customize with host names and titles. Files were exported as .MOGRTs from After Effects to be used in Premiere Pro.
