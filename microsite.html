Microsites

Microsites are sponsor branded webpages hosted on IGN that promote a contest, sweepstakes, or a new video series. Due to using Shortstack to build these out, note that I am limited in how much design freedom I have, such as column placements, row placement, buttons, sectioning, etc.


Live builds done by George Aguila using ShortStack.

Streamer Showdown - Kellogg's Froot Loops

IGN and Froot Loops partnered up for a Fortnite-centric miniseries titled Streamer Showdown, which had two new streamers each week go head to head in Fornite challenges. I designed the microsite that promoted the series, a poll that viewers could vote on what challenge the next set of streamers would do, IGN's other series Fortnite Tonight, and an everchanging header that would either portray next week's new streamers or silhouettes with question marks.


I made sure to keep the site a balance of Fortnite content and sponsor branding (Froot Loops) while staying within website safezones. This site was updated weekly to reflect updates to the streamers, showtimes, and the episode playlist.

The Umbrella Academy - Netflix

IGN and Netflix partnered up to promote the Umbrella Challenge, which challenges users to open and close an umbrella as many times as they can in 15 seconds. I was tasked to design an Umbrella Academy braded microsite that had the instructions, entry form, gallery of entries, and the trailer. Note: I did not do the stick figure illustrations seen below.
