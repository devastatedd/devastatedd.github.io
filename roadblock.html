IGN Homepage Roadblocks

Homepage roadblocks are IGN website takeovers where a client can have three different 1900x500 banners loop as the user infinitely scrolls down. These are typically accompanied by a 300x500 unit and/or a sponsored content block. Autoplay videos and embedded videos are sometimes included in these banners.
