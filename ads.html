General Ads for IGN

Below are some of the frequent client sponsored advertisements I do as part of the in-house Sales Design team at IGN. Clients hand off assets and direction to us, we use those files to create advertisements to run on IGN's website and/or social media channels, and then send it off for edits and approval.

MONITOR / SET GRAPHICS

Sponsor branded graphics for IGN livestreams and convention sets. Please note that some assets were made by other designers in collaboration, such as logos.

Blockbusters / Skins

Blockbusters have been retired due to the IGN homepage redesign and are now Trilogy Roadblocks.

CONTENT BLOCKS

Content blocks are branded ads on the IGN website that link through to a video or an article.

BIG REVEAL

Big Reveals are 1113 x 800px responsive units that appear on native article pages.

Snipes

Snipes are branded video graphics that appear for a few seconds on the bottom left corner of a video to showcase who is sponsoring it. Note that I cannot edit established branding, such as the red header. In rare cases, such as for new IGN shows, I can create a snipe template from scratch.

END CARDS

Sponsored end cards for Instagram and Facebook posts.
