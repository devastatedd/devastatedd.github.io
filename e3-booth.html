IGN Live at E3 - Booth Monitors

Designed and animated IGN statistic videos to loop on IGN's vertical booth monitors at E3 2017.


View the full animation here.

To play alongside the stats videos, I created a Top 10 List animation that would show the top 10 E3 games of each console during the convention. Each list was made into an easily editable template for on-site editors to adjust and render while keeping all animation untouched. Booth design done by Lunar Saloon


View the full Top 10 animation here.

In 2018, I got to revisit the animated lists and update it to match IGN's booth design for E3 2018. A golden frame was given to each list to mimic the showcase theme found throughout the booth. Booth design done by Lunar Saloon.
