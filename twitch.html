Twitch Channel Branding

Design and animation I did for my own personal Twitch channel's branding in 2021.
Below are my profile panels that I designed in Illustrator. I wanted something simple, consistent, and eyecatching.

For my Starting Soon screen, which plays at the start of my livestreams, I made vectors of my plushies that I display on my camera. These were then imported into After Effects where I did the motion graphics and created all the background assets. The full video is 48 seconds long!
