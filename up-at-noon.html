Up at Noon

Animated and designed video graphics for IGN's rebooted Up at Noon, a weekly pop culture variety show hosted by Brian Altano and Max Scoville.

-

Logo design and art direction: Justin Vachon

Animation and video graphics: Kathleen Oum

Animation support: Jack Taylor

Watch an episode below:


Intro

Once the finalized logo was created by Justin Vachon, I was given direction to make an intro that was 90s-Nickolodeon-meets-modern inspired with Double Dare and slime being the main influence. I used the paint splatter in the logo as my main source of inspiration for my first intro draft.

After feedback, motion blur, staggering, and touch ups to the logo aniamtion was added to create this finalized intro.

Transitions

Early transition drafts focused on the paint splatters covering the screen, but it was narrowed down to multicolored triangles from the logo and a wipe over the abstract memphis background from the intro.

Lower Thirds, Upper Thirds, and Namebars

Customizable lower and upper thirds were needed for easy use by the Video Team. This involved me animating it in after Effects and then preparing the lower thirds as responsive .MOGRT templates for Premiere Pro use.

Multiple name bars were created for use with up to 4 hosts. These namebars were templatized so that the video editor(s) could toggle on and off up to 4 namebars.

Snipe

Put together the snipe for future sponsored segments where a sponsor would need to be shown.

End Cards

End card for the sizzle and an end card for the regular episodes.
